About the game

The Adventures of Juanito Bayani is an immersive 3D roguelike, action-rpg that brings Philippine mythology to life. Players take on the role of Juanito, a young warrior on a mission to rescue his mother, Adeliada, who has been abducted by a powerful Sorcerer, the Hollow. As Juanito journeys through mystical lands, he must defeat the Hollow’s minions, uncover hidden clues, and face powerful generals before reaching his ultimate adversary.

Characters

Juanito Bayani

Juanito Bayani is the main protagonist of the game. He is a Juanito is a spirited and determined 18-year-old boy thrust into an unfamiliar and dangerous world. Initially naive and unsure of himself, he grows into a confident and courageous hero, driven by the desire to rescue his mother and uncover his family's legacy. As the main protagonist, Juanito wields the Agimat, a powerful artifact that grants him extraordinary abilities. He learns to combine combat skills, cultural knowledge, and mystical powers to overcome challenges. Juanito's primary motivation is to save his mother, Adelaida, and protect his village from the looming threats of mythical creatures.
